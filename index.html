Our Mission

Celebrating love
without the burden.

Project Shagun is a profound social initiative dedicated to supporting underprivileged couples. We believe that every couple deserves to celebrate their union with joy, respect, and dignity, regardless of their financial background.

By organizing beautifully curated mass weddings, we eliminate the exploitative expectations of dowry and the crippling debt that often follows traditional weddings. We provide a complete foundation for a new beginning.

  • Legal Registration

    Ensuring the marriage is officially recognized and protected under law.

  • Dignified Ceremony

    A beautiful, community-supported celebration with utmost respect.

"A celebration of community."
